Tech Scammers Are Targeting Senior Communities — Here Is What to Watch For

Mission Bay News Staff • May 2026

Phone and computer scams aimed at seniors have increased across Alameda County. Callers pose as Social Security agents, Medicare reps, and tech support workers from companies like Microsoft or Apple. They pressure residents to buy gift cards or allow remote access to their computers. No legitimate agency will ever ask for payment by gift card.

Know Your Rights as a Mobile Home Resident

California HCD • Reference

California residents in mobile home parks are protected under the Mobilehome Residency Law (MRL). You have rights around rent increases, eviction, park rules changes, and maintenance. Key state resources are listed in the sidebar.
